Comfortable Sitting Plane and CLasses

Sitting comfortably in class helps you stay focused and learn better. Choose a seat where you can see and hear the teacher clearly. Sit up straight with both feet flat on the floor to support your back. Take breaks to stand and stretch when allowed to keep your muscles relaxed.

Libarary

The school library is a wonderful place to read, study, and discover new information. It offers a quiet environment with comfortable seating to help you focus on your work. The library is stocked with a variety of books, magazines, and digital resources that support your learning and spark your curiosity.

Computer Labs

Computer labs in schools are essential for developing students' computer literacy and research skills, providing access to educational software, online resources, and collaborative learning opportunities.
